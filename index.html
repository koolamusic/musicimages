Collection Of Algorithmic Music Research Papers

Here is a Collection of 29 research papers on algorithmic music and conceptual design theory. All these papers can be found freely on google but I thought a single resource collection would be valuable to though wanting to read more on the subject.. If have any Papers on this or similar topic and like them shared here please contact me and I be more that willing to share them with others.
